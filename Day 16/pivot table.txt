📍 Topic: Pivot Table (pivot_table())

Welcome to Day 16! 🚀

Aaj hum Pandas ka ek bahut powerful feature seekhenge.

🔥 pivot_table()

Ye Excel ki Pivot Table ka Pandas version hai.

Data Analysts iska use reports banane ke liye roz karte hain.

🤔 Real-Life Example

Maan lo company ne sales data diya hai.

City	Product	Sales
Delhi	Laptop	50000
Delhi	Mouse	5000
Indore	Laptop	45000
Indore	Mouse	6000
Bhopal	Laptop	48000

Ab manager poochta hai:

Har city me har product ki average sales kitni hai?

Iske liye Pivot Table best hai.

Step 1 – Dataset
import pandas as pd

df = pd.DataFrame({
    "City": [
        "Delhi","Delhi","Indore",
        "Indore","Bhopal","Bhopal"
    ],
    "Product": [
        "Laptop","Mouse","Laptop",
        "Mouse","Laptop","Mouse"
    ],
    "Sales":[
        50000,5000,45000,
        6000,48000,5500
    ]
})

print(df)
1️⃣ Simple Pivot Table
print(pd.pivot_table(
    df,
    values="Sales",
    index="City"
))

Output

City	Sales
Bhopal	26750
Delhi	27500
Indore	25500

👉 Default function = mean()

2️⃣ Product-wise Pivot
print(pd.pivot_table(
    df,
    values="Sales",
    index="City",
    columns="Product"
))

Output

City	Laptop	Mouse
Delhi	50000	5000
Indore	45000	6000
Bhopal	48000	5500
3️⃣ Sum Instead of Mean
print(pd.pivot_table(
    df,
    values="Sales",
    index="City",
    aggfunc="sum"
))

Output

City	Sales
Delhi	55000
Indore	51000
Bhopal	53500
4️⃣ Count
print(pd.pivot_table(
    df,
    values="Sales",
    index="City",
    aggfunc="count"
))
5️⃣ Multiple Functions
print(pd.pivot_table(
    df,
    values="Sales",
    index="City",
    aggfunc=["mean", "max", "min"]
))
6️⃣ Fill Missing Values
print(pd.pivot_table(
    df,
    values="Sales",
    index="City",
    columns="Product",
    fill_value=0
))

Agar kisi city me koi product na ho to NaN ki jagah 0 aa jayega.

7️⃣ Margins (Grand Total)
print(pd.pivot_table(
    df,
    values="Sales",
    index="City",
    columns="Product",
    aggfunc="sum",
    margins=True
))

Output me ek extra row aur column All aayega.
